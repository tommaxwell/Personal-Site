Hi, I'm Tom!

Welcome to my little piece of the internetz.

I'm an engineer, entrepreneur, and a trailblazer -- please don't call me a "rockstar," and especially not a "ninja." Previously I was on the advisory board of Lockerz, a social commerce startup that rewards its users for sharing content from across the web (you should check it out). I like to believe that I have a strong business acumen, but I'm also passionate about front-end engineering. I code in some pretty familiar languages and frameworks like JavaScript, jQuery, CoffeeScript, and some other stuff. I'm also a hardcore nomad, so it's hard to catch me in one place for very long.

Before studying business or computer science, I was kind of a big deal on YouTube (not modest enough?). My technology-related videos garnered millions of views and thousands of subscribers before I decided that coding and startups were more important to me.
